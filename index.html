Wassila

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Information System at Towson University. I received my Ph.D from the University of Maryland Baltimore County and the University of Science and Technology Houari Boumedine. .    
      

My Research interests include Telehealth System, Cyber physical system security, Intelligent AI Attacks, Communication Privacy, Machine Learning for Communication, and distributed system.
